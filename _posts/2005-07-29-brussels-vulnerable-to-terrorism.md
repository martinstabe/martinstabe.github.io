Brussels governor **Veronique Paulus de Chatelet** has said [the seat of the European Union is under-prepared for a terrorist attack][1]. Just 22 of the city&rsquo;s 68 tube stations have emergency plans in place, and many so-called &ldquo;soft targets&rdquo; also have no emergency plans, she told Flemish radio.
